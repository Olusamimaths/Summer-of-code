About re:learn

At re:learn, our goal is to help schools use technology in a smart way to enhance learning.
Through content curation and capacity development - with a wide network of trained
facilitators, our aim is to improve the learning experience in and out of the classroom.

The re:learn Centre located in Abuja is a home of experimentation as we indentify innovative
ways to equip teachers and students with 21st century tools to accelerate the delivery of content
and ultimately students' learning.
